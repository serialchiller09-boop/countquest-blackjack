CountQuest Blackjack

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Overview

CountQuest Blackjack (“CountQuest”, “the app”, “we”) is an educational blackjack and card-counting trainer. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used. CountQuest is designed to work primarily on your device without requiring an account.

CountQuest uses simulated chips and gems only. It is not real-money gambling. No cash prizes are offered.

Information we store on your device

Game progress, settings, and statistics are saved locally in your browser or app storage (for example, via localStorage). This may include:

  • Training stats, ranks, bankroll, chips, and gems
  • Help level, sound preference, and gameplay settings
  • Drill scores, session history, and daily goal progress
  • Counting Crews data stored locally on your device (invite codes, crew chat, and related progress)
  • Optional VIP pass status and trial usage

This data stays on your device unless you export it yourself (for example, using the in-app “Export Stats” feature) or clear app/browser storage.

Information we do not collect by default

By default, CountQuest does not:

  • Run its own accounts or login servers
  • Upload your game save to our servers
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Sell your personal information

Optional third-party services

Some optional features may connect to third-party services if you choose to use or configure them:

  • Social sign-in (Google or Facebook) — if enabled in Settings, you may be redirected to the provider’s sign-in page. That provider’s privacy policy governs data they collect. CountQuest stores only a local “connected” status and related bonus flags on your device.
  • External payment links (web builds only) — if a payment link is configured for VIP features on a web deployment, checkout is handled by the payment provider (for example, Stripe). We do not receive your full payment card details.

The Android app requests Internet access so these optional flows and future online features can work. Core single-player gameplay does not require an account.

Children’s privacy

CountQuest is not directed at children under 13. Because the app involves simulated casino gameplay and card-counting training, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at j.pierson1990@outlook.com and we will take appropriate steps.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete locally stored data at any time by:

  • Using in-app “Reset Progress”
  • Clearing app data (Android) or site data (browser)
  • Uninstalling the app

Because progress is stored on your device, uninstalling or clearing data permanently removes it unless you have exported a backup.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when updates are posted. Continued use of CountQuest after changes means you accept the updated policy.
